# Slide 47 of Lecture 2 contains information about Involved actors in a transportation process (see module 10)
There are nearly 100 different types of supporting services involved in a transportation process, but some typical examples include: (1) export/import managers, (2) quota agents, traders or merchants, (3) freight forwarders, (4) customs brokers and other solution providers, (5) carriers or shipping companies, (6) NVOCCs, (7) goods surveyors, (8) container leasing companies, (9) export management companies, (10) export trading companies and (11) own-account carriage and third-party logistics (3PL) providers.
