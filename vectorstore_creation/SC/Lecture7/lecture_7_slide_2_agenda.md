# Slide 2 of Lecture 7 contains information about Agenda

TOPICS OF TODAY
Today’s topics cover the supra-national legal framework of trade, including economic integration levels and trade facilitation, as well as what you need to know for export and import operations. In addition, the lecture introduces Lex Mercatoria and trade terms, looking in particular at international procedural law and the law of sales contracts, and at Incoterms® 2020. The readings for this session are Textbook 4th ed., Chapter 9; Grainger, A. (2011), “Trade Facilitation: A Conceptual Review”, Journal of World Trade, Vol. 45, No. 1, pp. 39–62; and TFG (2020), “Incoterms® 2020 Rules”, Trade Finance Global (`https://www.tradefinanceglobal.com/incoterms/`).
