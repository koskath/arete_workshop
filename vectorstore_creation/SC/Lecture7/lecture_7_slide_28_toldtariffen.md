# Slide 28 of Lecture 7 contains information about Toldtariffen:

Danish National Customs Tariff Toldtariffen, the integrated tariff environment - is designed for anyone who deals with trade in goods between the EU and third countries. The system provides accurate and updated data on the EU's Common Customs Tariff and national measures via the Internet.
With this Customs Tariff, it is possible to make web-based queries, customs calculations, generate transcripts and disseminate data. See: http://tarif.skat.dk/tariff/index.jsf
