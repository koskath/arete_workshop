# Slide 38 of Lecture 10 contains information about the References.

Key references for this lecture include *Building Machine Learning and Deep Learning Models on Google Cloud Platform* by E. Bisong, *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow* by Aurélien Géron, and *Deep Learning* by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville (The MIT Press).

Additional sources are “TensorFlow and Clipper” (Lecture 24, CS262a) by Ali Ghodsi and Ion Stoica at UC Berkeley in 2018, “Large-Scale Deep Learning with TensorFlow” by Jeff Dean and the Google Brain team, and the project listings at https://projects.apache.org/projects.html.
