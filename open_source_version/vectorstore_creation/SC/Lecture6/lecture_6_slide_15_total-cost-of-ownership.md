### Column Definitions
**Activity Related to the Purchasing Process** describes the stage of the purchasing process (pretransaction, transaction, posttransaction) and the specific activity performed.

**Potential Cost Elements** lists the types of costs that arise from each activity (e.g., capital requisition, price paid, downtime).

**Cost Drivers** explains the underlying factors that cause or influence those costs (e.g., labor, documents, equipment movement).

---

## Row-by-Row Description

### PRETRANSACTION

**Row: Need for new production equipment identified**  
Activity: Identifying the need for new equipment.  
Potential Cost Elements: Capital requisition; form committee to investigate.  
Cost Drivers: Creating and filing documents; salaries of committee members.

**Row: Form design team**  
Activity: Creating a team to evaluate and design solutions.  
Potential Cost Elements: Team costs; identifying external candidates such as potential suppliers.  
Cost Drivers: Salaries of team members; costs of identifying, contacting, and meeting candidates.

---

### TRANSACTION

**Row: Equipment Purchased**  
Activity: Buying the equipment.  
Potential Cost Elements: Price paid.  
Cost Drivers: Cost of equipment; delivery; warranty.

**Row: Equipment Installation**  
Activity: Installing the equipment.  
Potential Cost Elements: Installing equipment; shutting down other operations.  
Cost Drivers: Labor, moving old equipment, raw materials, losses from destroyed/tested items, finished goods shortages, and labor if paid for idle time.

---

### POSTTRANSACTION

**Row: Routine Maintenance**  
Activity: Conducting regular maintenance.  
Potential Cost Elements: Downtime; cost of warranty or out-of-warranty work.  
Cost Drivers: Labor downtime; inventory buildup or shortages; price paid for work performed.

**Row: Repairs**  
Activity: Repairing the equipment after issues arise.  
Potential Cost Elements: Downtime; cost of warranty or out-of-warranty work.  
Cost Drivers: Labor downtime; inventory buildup or shortages; price paid for repair work.
