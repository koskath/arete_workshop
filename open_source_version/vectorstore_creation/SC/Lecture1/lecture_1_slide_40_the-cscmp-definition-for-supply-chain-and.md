# Slide 40 of Lecture 1 contains information about The CSCMP definition for supply chain and SCM
CSCMP Definition of Supply Chain Management: Supply Chain Management encompasses the planning and management of activities involved in sourcing, procurement, conversion, and all Logistics Management activities. It highlights the importance of coordination and collaboration with channel partners, which can include suppliers, intermediaries, third-party service providers, and customers. It concludes by stating that Supply Chain Management integrates supply and demand management within and across companies.
