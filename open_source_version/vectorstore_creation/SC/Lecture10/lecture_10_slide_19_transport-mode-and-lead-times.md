# Slide 19 of Lecture 10 contains information about Transport Mode and Lead Times

Lead times are closely related to decisions on transport mode.

A valuable strategy is accounting logistics costs on a full-cost basis.
- Freight, insurance and handling costs
- Inventory carrying costs for products in transit and cycle stock
- Inventory carrying costs for safety stock
- Investment cost required to produce the inventory to fill the pipeline
