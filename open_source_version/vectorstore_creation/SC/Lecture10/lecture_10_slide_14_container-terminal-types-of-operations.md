# Slide 14 of Lecture 10 contains information about Container Terminal Types of Operations

## Reach Stacker System
(on-side transports by terminal tractors)

# Here is what the image contains:
- A crane with a container being moved.
- Sea-side Transhipment area where containers are offloaded.
- Temporary Storage area with stacked containers.
- Land-side Transhipment area.
- Storage capacity around 500 TEU per hectare.

## Pure Straddle Carrier System
- Crane with container transfer system.
- Sea-side Transhipment area.
- Temporary Storage area with containers.
- Land-side Transhipment area.
- Storage capacity 500 to 750 TEU per hectare.

## Rubber Tired Gantry Crane System
(on-side transports by terminal tractors)

# Here is what the image contains:
- Crane operating over stacked containers.
- Sea-side Transhipment area.
- Temporary Storage area with numerous containers.
- Land-side Transhipment area.
- Storage capacity about 1,000 TEU per hectare.

## Rail Mounted Gantry Crane System
(on-side transports by straddle carriers)

# Here is what the image contains:
- Crane with rail-mounted system.
- Sea-side Transhipment area.
- Temporary Storage area with containers.
- Land-side Transhipment area.
- Storage capacity about 1,100 TEU per hectare.
