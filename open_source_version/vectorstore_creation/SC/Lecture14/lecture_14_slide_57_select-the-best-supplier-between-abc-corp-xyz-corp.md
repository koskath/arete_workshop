# Slide 57 of Lecture 14 contains information about Select the Best Supplier Between ABC Corp. & XYZ Corp.

## Supplier Data
### Supplier 1: **ABC Corp.**
- **Quoted price (without any extras):** $10.00  
- **Freight costs in 2007:** You incurred a total freight cost of **$19** for sourcing **100 pieces** using **road & water** modes.  
- **Discounts:** Offers **2% discount** on prompt payment.  
- **Delivery performance:** Delivers the product **85% on time**.  
- **Quality:** You rejected **13%** of ABC’s goods last year.  
- **Lead time:** Takes **7 weeks** to source the product.  
- **Recycling policy:** ABC does **not recycle** and does **not fully comply** with your recycling policy.  
- **Heroics:** Has **never** gone out of the way to help in times of need or distress.  
- **Background:** A typical American supplier from the Midwest, but the owner and you both attended **Copenhagen Business School**.

### Supplier 2: **XYZ Corp.**
- **Quoted price (without any extras):** $12.00  
- **Freight costs in 2007:** You incurred a total freight cost of **$50** for sourcing **150 pieces** using a mix of **truck & air** transport.  
- **Discounts:** Offers **1% discount** on prompt payment.  
- **Delivery performance:** Delivers the product **100% on time**.  
- **Quality:** There were **no rejects** from XYZ’s goods last year.  
- **Lead time:** Takes **10 weeks** to source the product.  
- **Recycling policy:** XYZ **recycles** and fully complies with your recycling and **CSR** policies.  
- **Heroics:** Has actively helped in difficult circumstances:  
  - When a **fire** destroyed your stock  
  - During a **shortage**  
  - When a **shipment was lost**  
- **Background:** An atypical American supplier; the owner is **Danish** and has been living and working in the US for **7 years**.
