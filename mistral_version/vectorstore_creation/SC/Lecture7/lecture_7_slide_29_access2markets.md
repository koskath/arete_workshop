# Slide 29 of Lecture 7 contains information about Access2Markets

Access2Markets is a website run by the Directorate‑General for Trade (DG‑Trade) of the European Commission that provides information to companies exporting from the EU about import conditions in third‑country markets. It offers details on duties and taxes on imports of products into specific countries, as well as the procedures and documents required for customs clearance in the partner country. The site also contains statistics on trade flows in goods between EU and non‑EU countries, information on trade barriers affecting exports, and guidance on food safety, animal health and plant health measures. In addition, it covers preferential agreements and rules of origin, services for small and medium‑sized enterprises (SMEs) and more general country trade information. More about Access2Markets can be found at `https://trade.ec.europa.eu/access-to-markets/en/content/`. 29
