# Slide 18 of Lecture 2 contains information about Global competition for capital value based
Management – what is the role of logistics and SCM in that picture? According to Kenichi Ohno, the post-industrial period (from the late 1980s) is one in which the “arteries” of the economy are “cleansed” of “sclerotic” nation states, and capital flows freely and unconstrained. This leads to a focus on the management of capital flows and to re-asking all activities with respect to their value contribution.

As a consequence, companies concentrate on core competencies and outsource non-core competencies, often with a strong emphasis on “headcount”.
