# Slide 36 of Lecture 10 contains information about Fourth Party Logistics (4PLs ®)

Offering total outsource supply chain solutions  
Originally trademarked by Accenture in 1996  
• A supply chain integrator that assembles and manages the resources, capabilities and technology of its own organisation, with those complementary logistics service providers, to deliver a comprehensive supply chain solution  
• Actually asset-free, independent logistics intermediary  

But the line between 3PL and 4PL ® is now blurred as a lot of 3PLs now offer 4PL ® type solutions, too.  
Kühne&Nagel  
e.g. claims to be a Lead Logistics Provider (LLP)
