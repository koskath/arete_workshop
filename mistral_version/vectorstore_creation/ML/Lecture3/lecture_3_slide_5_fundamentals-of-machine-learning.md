# Slide 5 of Lecture 3 contains information about the Fundamentals of Machine Learning.

To excel at chess there are three approaches: Symbolic AI proposes sitting down with Magnus Carlsen to encode his expertise directly into a program, Statistical AI favors simulating every possible move and outcome to choose the most likely path to victory, and the Machine Learning approach feeds the program millions of real and simulated games, both wins and losses, so it can learn from experience.
